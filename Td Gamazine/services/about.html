TD GAMAZINE

Company History

TD Gamazine Supply (Pty) Ltd is an existing small- size construction and services company. It is a 100% BEE (Black Economic Empowerment) company. The core business of this company is to render Gamazine and construction services. TD Gamazine Supply (Pty) Ltd is 100% owned by a historical disadvantaged and young person. It was founded in 2023 in Ivory Park (Gauteng) province. Mr Thulani Dube is the founder of this company. He has started this company in order to create employment opportunities for himself and for other unemployed members of the community, with emphasis on woman, youth and people with disability TD Gamazine Supply (Pty) Ltd was legally registered in 2023 as PTY (LTD) with CIPRO (Company and Intellectual Property Registration Office)to make it a legal entity and to give it a corporate image. It is also registered for income Tax with South African Revenue Services (SARS) TD Gamazine Supply Ltd is 100% owned and managed by Mr Thulani Dube
